---
title: 'Set your Fapshi PIN'
description: 'How do I set my Fapshi PIN?'
keywords: ['set pin', 'fapshi pin', 'account security', 'fapshi account', 'pin verification']
---

To set your Fapshi PIN, follow these steps:

1. Log in to your Fapshi account.
2. Navigate to the "Security" section.
3. Click on "Set Fapshi PIN."
4. Enter your desired PIN and confirm it.
5. Save your changes.

Your Fapshi PIN is now set! Make sure to remember it for future transactions.
